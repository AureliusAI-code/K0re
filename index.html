V0ID

A mineable store of value. Forge V0IDLINGS. Strike the motherlode. Burn your way to scarcity.

1. What is V0ID

V0ID is a BASE-native token where every whole V0ID you hold is paired 1:1 with a V0IDLING — a fully on-chain pixel entity with a class, seed hash, and mining power.

Buying V0ID through the canonical Uniswap v4 pool automatically mints V0IDLINGS to your wallet. Selling burns them. Transfers move the paired V0IDLINGS together with the token.

On top of the token + NFT layer, V0ID introduces a self-contained mining economy. Stake your V0IDLINGS to earn ongoing V0ID emissions. Large buys gain access to the motherlode jackpot.

3. V0IDLINGS — the embedded NFT

V0IDLINGS are not a separate ERC-721 collection. Every whole V0ID automatically owns one V0IDLING. When V0ID moves, V0IDLINGS move. When V0ID burns, V0IDLINGS burn.

Each V0IDLING has:

  • Class — Mortal, Hero, Titan, God
  • Hash — determines Mining Power
  • Eyes — glow, visor, goggles
  • Crown — halo, antenna, cap
  • Tool — pickaxe, drill, gem cluster
  • Background — neon color variants

4. Staking and mining rewards

Stake a V0IDLING and begin earning V0ID emissions. Your reward share is proportional to Mining Power relative to all staked V0IDLINGS.

Rewards accrue continuously. The dashboard updates pending rewards live in real time.

CLAIM takes a 10% refined-ore tax redistributed to remaining stakers.

5. The motherlode lottery

Every qualifying buy ≥ 0.1 BASE receives a chance to hit the motherlode jackpot.

0.1 BASE buy 0.16%
0.5 BASE buy 0.53%
1 BASE buy 1.00%

On a hit, the motherlode pool splits 50 / 50:

  • 50% → buyer
  • 50% → random staker
